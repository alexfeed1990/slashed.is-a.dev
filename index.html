Born in Romania, I started programming when I was around 7yrs old. I started in Batch, the most basic programming language and I made basic TUI (terminal UI) programs.

Next, I moved onto Roblox Studio, which is where I learned Lua. It is an amazing language which I can recommend to beginners. I stuck to Roblox for a few years but I never ended up making something that was very successful.

While I was messing around in Roblox Studio, I was also messing around with HTML. I was making basic websites with little to no Javascript as it looked so complicated to me.

Then, I started making Discord bots in Python. JS was still very daunting to me, but I soon discovered the power of it as a friend recommended me Discord.JS

This is where my NodeJS career starts. My very first program in NodeJS was a Discord bot made with the help of discordjs.guide. I made a few other bots after that, my biggest being MemeBot, a shameless copy of Dank Memer.

Around 2021, I was introduced to Deno. The "successor" to NodeJS. And to be honest, it was great. But TypeScript was a pain in the ass. It introduced the need for relentless boilerplate when you're just trying to make something simple. TypeScript helps with organization in large-scale projects, but it is very annoying in a small project.

Around this time I was also making small games in Unity, with C#. I never really finished any of them, but I also have some skill in WPF and WinForms (which nobody uses).
